Our Story

Three Decades of
Campaign Excellence

Founded in 1991, Satish Art has been the trusted backbone of election campaigns across India. We specialize in producing high-quality promotional materials that connect political parties with their voters.

Our vision was simple — affordable, durable, and visually powerful campaign materials. Today we serve clients from local panchayat to national parties.
